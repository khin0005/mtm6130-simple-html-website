How to Choose the Best Mattress Based on How You Sleep

Getting the right mattress can really improve how well you sleep and how your body feels in the morning. Everyone’s different, so it depends on how you sleep and what feels good to you.

Side Sleepers

  • Go for a soft to medium mattress
  • It should cushion your shoulders and hips
  • Memory foam or a soft hybrid works well

Back Sleepers

  • Choose a medium-firm mattress
  • It should support your spine and keep it straight
  • Hybrid or firm memory foam is a good choice
